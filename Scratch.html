Swimming: The Sport You Never Knew About

  1. Butterfly: The "I wish I was dead" stroke. Why? Because...
    • Uses all muscles possible
    • Water splashed in your mouth when you try to breathe
    • Most tiring stroke


  2. Breaststroke: The "I look like a dying seal" stroke. Why it's my favorite:
    • Feels SO COOL when you get it smooth
    • The technique is very difficult, but satisfying, too


  3. Freestyle: The "Don't race it unless you're REALLY good" stroke. Why? Because...
    • Everyone and their dog wants to race it, so you'd better be serious about it
    • Easiest stroke to simply "do", so in order to stand out, you must be exceptional


What inspired me to become a swimmer

I have always been an avid swimmer and although I may have a love/hate relationship from the early practices to the never ending workouts, I hope this website and the information provided will help others who may not know much about the sport of swimming to come to understand and appreciate the intensity, hard work, and dedication needed in order to be a swimmer.
